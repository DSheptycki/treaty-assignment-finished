Why are Treaties Important Today?

Modern treaties establish clearly defined land ownership and jurisdiction, and foster a new relationship based on mutual respect, trust, and understanding - BCTreaty. In this day in age First Nations peoples and all canadians as a whole are coming together and progressing as humans, These treaties today serve as ground rules to keep everyone in line and to aid in humanity becoming equal. Treaty 1 land is shared by all peoples and is constantly being progressed with brand new real-estate and landmarks and will help path a future towards a better tommorow.
